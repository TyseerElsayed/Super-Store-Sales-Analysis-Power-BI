I built a Super Store Sales Dashboard using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior across different dimensions.

The dashboard provides a clear view of:
Overall business performance (Sales, Profit, Orders)
Sales distribution across categories and sub-categories
Monthly profit trends and seasonality
Customer segments and payment behavior
Geographic performance by state
Return rates and shipping insights

One of the key takeaways was identifying how certain categories drive high sales but lower profitability, along with noticeable fluctuations in monthly profit — including a negative dip that highlights potential inefficiencies.
